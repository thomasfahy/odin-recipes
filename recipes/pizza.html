Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a globally adored dish that originated in Italy, characterized by its thin, crispy crust and vibrant toppings. Traditionally, it features a base of tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese

Beyond its delicious flavors, pizza holds a special place in social dining, often shared among friends and family during gatherings. It can be found in a variety of styles, such as the thick, hearty slices of Chicago deep-dish or the foldable

Ingredients

  • 600g flour
  • 500g tomato pasata
  • 1 garlic
  • chopped ham
  • 400g mozarella cheese
  • grated cheddar cheese
  • other preffered toppings

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough: Roll out store-bought pizza dough on a floured surface until it reaches your desired thickness. Place it on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  2. Add the Sauce: Spread a layer of tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  3. Top with Cheese: Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  4. Add Toppings: Arrange your favorite toppings, such as pepperoni, sliced vegetables, or olives, over the cheese.
  5. Bake: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and bake the pizza for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. Let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.
