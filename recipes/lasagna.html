Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a quintessential Italian comfort food, known for its layers of rich ingredients and robust flavors. Each layer typically consists of wide pasta sheets, a hearty meat or vegetable sauce

The versatility of lasagna allows for endless variations, catering to different dietary preferences and culinary creativity. Traditional recipes feature a meat-based Bolognese sauce, while vegetarian versions might include a medley of roasted vegetables and spinach. Whether served as a centerpiece for a family dinner or a dish for special occasions, lasagnas comforting and indulgent nature makes it a beloved choice in kitchens around the world.

  • 500g mince meet
  • 1 garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 400g cheddar cheese
  • 200g chopped tomatos
