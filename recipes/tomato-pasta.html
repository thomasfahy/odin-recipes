Tomato Pasta Recipe

Description

Tomato pasta is a classic Italian dish beloved for its simplicity and rich flavors. The essence of the dish lies in its tomato sauce, often made from ripe tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, and fresh basil,

Beyond its basic ingredients, tomato pasta serves as a canvas for culinary creativity. Home cooks and chefs alike often enhance the dish with additional elements such as fresh vegetables, meats, or cheeses

Ingredients

  • 400g Pasta
  • 500g chopped tomatos
  • 1 garlic
  • 1 onion
  • fresh basil

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta: Boil a pot of salted water and cook your preferred pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Sauce Base: In a large pan, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add Tomatoes: Add a can of crushed tomatoes or chopped fresh tomatoes to the pan. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of sugar to balance the acidity.
  4. Simmer: Let the sauce simmer for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly and the flavors meld together.
  5. combine and Serve: Toss the cooked pasta with the tomato sauce in the pan. Mix well to coat the pasta evenly. Serve with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil, if desired.
